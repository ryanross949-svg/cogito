About MORGAN

Methodology and design principles.

MORGAN is a cognitive training simulator designed to install the strategic thinking patterns used by elite investors and behavioral economists. It trains you to survive volatility, recognize asymmetric bets, and compound resources over time.

// Core Pedagogy

The system uses four evidence-based learning mechanisms, applied in concert:

  • Spaced Repetition (SM-2): Cards reappear at expanding intervals based on recall quality.
  • Active Recall: Every drill requires generation, not recognition.
  • Interleaving: Drills mix concepts across modules.
  • Elaboration: Arena problems and Project Asymmetry require applying concepts to novel scenarios.

// The Ten Axes

  • COMPOUND — The Eighth Wonder: Compound interest, time horizons
  • RISK — Risk vs. Volatility: Permanent loss of capital
  • ASYMM — Asymmetric Bets: Expected value, fat tails
  • PSYCH — Psychology of Money: Loss aversion, sunk cost
  • COST — Opportunity Cost: Discount rates, time value
  • MARGIN — Margin of Safety: Downside protection
  • CASH — Cash Flow: Liquidity, solvency, runway
  • CYCLE — Market Cycles: Mean reversion, contrarianism
  • TAX — Tax Efficiency: Friction, after-tax returns
  • MOAT — Economic Moats: Competitive advantage, pricing power
