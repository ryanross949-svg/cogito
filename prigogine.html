About PRIGOGINE

Methodology and design principles.

PRIGOGINE is a cognitive training simulator designed to install the strategic thinking patterns used by Ilya Prigogine and complexity scientists. It trains you to understand how complex order emerges not despite entropy, but because of it, through the dissipation of energy.

// Core Pedagogy

The system uses four evidence-based learning mechanisms, applied in concert:

  • Spaced Repetition (SM-2): Cards reappear at expanding intervals based on recall quality.
  • Active Recall: Every drill requires generation, not recognition.
  • Interleaving: Drills mix concepts across modules.
  • Elaboration: Arena problems and Project Negentropy require applying concepts to novel scenarios.
