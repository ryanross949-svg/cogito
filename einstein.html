About EINSTEIN

Methodology and design principles.

EINSTEIN is a cognitive training simulator designed to install the thinking patterns used by Albert Einstein and modern physicists to understand relativity and spacetime. It is not a textbook. It is a workout.

// Core Pedagogy

The system uses four evidence-based learning mechanisms, applied in concert:

  • Spaced Repetition (SM-2): Cards reappear at expanding intervals based on recall quality. Forgetting curve combat.
  • Active Recall: Every drill requires generation, not recognition. You must produce the answer, not just identify it.
  • Interleaving: Drills mix concepts across modules. Forced discrimination strengthens memory vs. blocked practice.
  • Elaboration: Arena problems and Project GALILEO require applying concepts to novel scenarios.

// The Four Axes

  • SPEED — The constancy of the speed of light and its consequences.
  • DILATION — Time dilation and length contraction.
  • EQUIVALENCE — The equivalence of gravity and acceleration.
  • GEOMETRY — Spacetime curvature and general relativity.
