About HEBB

Methodology and design principles.

HEBB is a cognitive training simulator designed to install the biological mechanisms of neuroplasticity. It teaches you how your brain physically rewires when you learn, allowing you to intentionally hack your own learning velocity.

// Core Pedagogy

The system uses four evidence-based learning mechanisms, applied in concert:

  • Spaced Repetition (SM-2): Cards reappear at expanding intervals based on recall quality.
  • Active Recall: Every drill requires generation, not recognition.
  • Interleaving: Drills mix concepts across modules.
  • Elaboration: Arena problems and Project Synapse require applying concepts to novel scenarios.

// The Ten Axes

  • WIRE — Hebbian learning ("neurons that fire together wire together")
  • PRUNE — Synaptic pruning ("use it or lose it")
  • INSULATE — Myelination (white matter and signal speed)
  • GENERATE — Neurogenesis (adult brain cell creation)
  • ROUTINE — Basal ganglia (habit formation)
  • EXECUTE — Prefrontal cortex (executive function)
  • REWARD — Dopamine (reward prediction error)
  • SLEEP — Memory consolidation
  • STRESS — Cortisol and hippocampal damage
  • ENRICH — BDNF and enriched environments
