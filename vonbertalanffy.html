About VON BERTALANFFY

Methodology and design principles.

VON BERTALANFFY is a cognitive training simulator designed to install the thinking patterns of General Systems Theory. It trains you to see the exact same mathematical patterns in a biological cell, a corporation, and a galaxy.

// The Ten Axes

  • ISOMORPHISM — Why different systems obey the same math
  • OPENNESS — Open vs. closed systems
  • STEADY — Dynamic equilibrium and homeostasis
  • EQUFINAL — Reaching the same state from different starts
  • FEEDBACK — Positive and negative loops
  • HIERARCHY — Holons and emergent properties
  • BOUNDARY — System interfaces
  • ENTROPY — Negentropy and importing order
  • COUPLING — Tight vs. loose system coupling
  • GESTALT — The whole is greater than the sum
