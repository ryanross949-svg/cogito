About LE BON

Methodology and design principles.

LE BON is a cognitive training simulator designed to install the strategic thinking patterns required to understand and navigate crowd psychology, memetics, and social contagion. It extends individual rationality to the group level.

// Core Pedagogy

The system uses four evidence-based learning mechanisms, applied in concert:

  • Spaced Repetition (SM-2): Cards reappear at expanding intervals based on recall quality.
  • Active Recall: Every drill requires generation, not recognition.
  • Interleaving: Drills mix concepts across modules.
  • Elaboration: Arena problems and Project Pandemic require applying concepts to novel scenarios.

// The Ten Axes

  • AXIOM — The Collective Mind (Why a crowd of intelligent individuals acts like a primitive organism)
  • CONTAGION — Emotional Contagion (How emotions spread faster than logic)
  • ANON — Deindividuation (Loss of self, anonymity, responsibility diffusion)
  • SUGGEST — Suggestibility (Hypnotic state of crowds, mirroring)
  • MEME — Meme Propagation (Dawkins\' memes, virality, replication)
  • SWARM — Swarm Logic (Emergence, flocking, decentralized coordination)
  • EXTREME — Polarization (Groupshift, echo chambers, radicalization)
  • LEADER — The Demagogue (Affirmation, repetition, prestige)
  • CULT — Cult Dynamics (Love bombing, isolation, charismatic control)
  • IMMUNE — Psychological Immunity (Inoculation, critical thinking, solitude)
