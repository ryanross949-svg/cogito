About BOLTZMANN

Methodology and design principles.

BOLTZMANN is a cognitive training simulator designed to install the thermodynamic thinking patterns used to understand life, energy, and the universe. It trains you to see the world as a flow of energy gradients fighting against the inevitable march of entropy.

// Core Pedagogy

The system uses four evidence-based learning mechanisms, applied in concert:

  • Spaced Repetition (SM-2): Cards reappear at expanding intervals based on recall quality.
  • Active Recall: Every drill requires generation, not recognition.
  • Interleaving: Drills mix concepts across modules.
  • Elaboration: Arena problems and Project Dissipation require applying concepts to novel scenarios.

// The Ten Axes

  • ARROW — The Arrow of Time, 2nd Law, heat death
  • ENTROPY — Microstates, macrostates, disorder vs uncertainty
  • GRADIENT — Energy gradients, heat flow, equilibrium
  • DISSIPATION — Dissipative structures, turbulence, friction
  • EMERGE — Emergent order, how life creates local order
  • METABOL — Metabolism, energy consumption, homeostasis
  • ATTRACT — Attractors, steady states, homeostasis
  • SYNERGY — Symbiosis, autocatalysis, hypercycles
  • EVOLVE — Evolution as entropy dissipation
  • MAXENT — Maximum entropy production, cosmic expansion
