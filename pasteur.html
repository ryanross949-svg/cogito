About PASTEUR

Methodology and design principles.

PASTEUR is a cognitive training simulator designed to install the empirical thinking patterns used by biologists, doctors, and scientists. It is not a textbook. It is a workout.

// Core Pedagogy

The system uses four evidence-based learning mechanisms, applied in concert:

  • Spaced Repetition (SM-2): Cards reappear at expanding intervals based on recall quality. Forgetting curve combat.
  • Active Recall: Every drill requires generation, not recognition. You must produce the answer, not just identify it.
  • Interleaving: Drills mix concepts across modules. Forced discrimination strengthens memory vs. blocked practice.
  • Elaboration: Arena problems and Project PANDEMIA require applying concepts to novel scenarios.

// The Ten Axes

  • PREPARE — The prepared mind
  • CONTROL — Rigorous experimental design
  • FALSIFY — Disproving dogma
  • CAUSE — Germ theory & causation
  • WEAKEN — Attenuation & vaccines
  • REPLICATE — Reproducibility
  • BIAS — Cognitive errors in science
  • ANOMALY — Signal vs noise
  • MECHANISM — Correlation vs causation
  • CURE — Translation to application
