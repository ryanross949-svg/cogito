About CHOMSKY

Methodology and design principles.

CHOMSKY is a cognitive training simulator designed to install the linguistic and structural thinking patterns used by Noam Chomsky and cognitive scientists. It trains you to parse syntax, understand recursion, and see how meaning is constructed from finite rules.

// Core Pedagogy

Spaced Repetition (SM-2), Active Recall, Interleaving, and Elaboration applied to linguistic structures.

// The Ten Axes

  • RECURSE — The infinite use of finite means
  • SYNTAX — Phrase structure and trees
  • UNIGRAM — Universal Grammar and innateness
  • SEMANT — Compositionality and meaning
  • PRAGMAT — Context, implicature, and Grice
  • MORPH — Morphemes and word formation
  • PHONOL — Sound patterns and phonemes
  • COGNIT — Language and thought (Sapir-Whorf)
  • NEURO — The brain\'s language centers
  • ACQUIRE — How children learn language
